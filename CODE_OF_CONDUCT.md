# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project aims to be a respectful, focused place to collaborate.

Examples of behavior that help:

- being clear, patient, and constructive
- assuming good intent while discussing technical tradeoffs
- keeping feedback specific and tied to the code, docs, or behavior
- respecting project scope and maintainer decisions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, personal attacks, insults, or discrimination
- trolling, baiting, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- repeated off-scope demands after a maintainer has said no
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not match this standard. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you run into a serious conduct issue, open a private security report or contact the maintainer through the repository contact path that is available at the time.

## Practical note

This is a small project. The expectation is simple: be respectful, stay on scope, and make collaboration easier rather than harder.
